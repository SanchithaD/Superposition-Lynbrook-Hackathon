## FAQs
<List id="faq">

How can I help out with Superposition Lynbrook Hackathon?
- Superposition Lynbrook is always looking for judges, sponsors, and partners! Please reach out to us at ch.lynbrookhs@superposition.tech or through our hackathon slack at TBD

Who can apply? What if I'm in middle school?
 - Although our event is geared towards high school students, we welcome middle school students too! 
 
When and Where is Superposition Lynbrook Hackathon happening?
- Superposition Lynbrook happens on the weekend April 1-2, 2023 at Lynbrook high school

Where can I contact organizers?
- You can contact anyone in our team through our hackathon slack at or through our team email ch.lynbrookhs@superposition.tech
    
What do I bring?
- An idea, and enthusiasm is all that's needed! If you have one, we recommend bringing your laptop. We will be providing food during the event.

What if I don't have a team or idea?
- Superposition Lynbrook Hackathon will have workshops and team building time/activities where you'll have the chance to come up with an idea or make a team.
